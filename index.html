CREATING HIGH-PERFORMANCE TEAMS CONFERENCE BOGOTÁ 2022

This event aims to bring together business leaders and teams who want to learn and understand how teamwork is managed around the world for three days of exciting talks and impressive presentations.

2022.02.18(SAT)- 20(MON)

Gran Salon Corferias, Bogotá-Colombia

Main Program

Lecture

We will present global trends and developments and feature articles of interest that reflect the current state of design.

Exhibition

Exhibition of works in different finishes by Colombian leads who have participated in different fairs around the world.

Forums

Designers from various contries speaking about their experiences and new trends.

Worshop

We will do a practical workshop, where we will explore the most used techniques for design thinking.

CHPT Feedback

We will make a small feedback in figures of these years of activity and how the design movement has grown and we will see how it has grown.
